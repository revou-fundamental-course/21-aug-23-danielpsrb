Profile SMA NEGERI 17 MEDAN

Visi

Unggul dalam prestasi, yang Bermartabat berdasarkan IMTAQ dan Berbasis lingkungan

Misi

  1. Menyelenggarakan proses pembelajaran dan bimbingan konseling secara efektif
  2. Menegakkan kedisiplinan yang religious Bermartabat dan beretika bagi semua warga sekolah
  3. Memberdayakan potensi sekolah untuk melengkapi sarana dan prasarana serta administrasi guna mengembangkan aktifitas dan ktreatifitas yang bermutu serta berwawasan lingkungan.

Our Facilitation

SMA Negeri 17 Medan provides adequate facilities to support learning activities and meet the needs of students and teachers, for example classrooms, biology laboratories, computer labs, sports halls, canteens, and others

Professional & Experienced Teacher

The team of teachers and instructors at SMA Negeri 17 Medan are individuals who are very professional and experienced in their respective fields. They have a strong commitment to teaching, educating, and mentoring students

Sport Field

the sports hall owned by SMA Negeri 17 Medan provides modern tools and equipment for exercising, and has a sports field that is quite wide and spacious

Classroom

SMAN 17 Medan has a total of 24 classrooms consisting of 8 class X classrooms, 8 class XI classrooms, and 8 class XII classrooms

Biology Lab

Our biology laboratory is a place to carry out experimental practice for students, supported by adequate and sufficient tools and equipment for carrying out practice

Canteen

at SMA 17 Medan there are 3 canteens with a variety of food and drinks to reduce the hunger of students during break times, these three canteens also offer various prices for food and drinks

Computer-Lab

The computer and language laboratory at SMAN 17 Medan has multi-functions starting from computer learning for students, conducting computer-based exams, and being used for state high school entrance selection tests.
